  • About Heimdall

Project Heimdall utilizes drone technology to assist in livestock management. With the Heimdall drone system livestock may be located, observed, and managed as needed by the user. The Heimdall system includes self- protection protocols built into the drone's operating systems which are able to detect possible dangers to the drone units. These self-protection protocols include battery life monitoring, external sensors, and location tracking on all Heimdall drone units. Also, all Heimdall drones come equipped with 1080p camera's which are capable of capturing video and streaming live video feeds to the users device.

Drone technology provides new mechanical means of completing daily tasks and assignments. By utilizing technology time can be saved and a more productive operation is established.
